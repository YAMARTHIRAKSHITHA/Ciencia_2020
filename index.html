About the Event

CIENCIA ,

A National Level Technical Fest Organized by CVR College of Engineering every year.
This grand celeration of innovation gives students from all the country an opurtunity to show case their talent and ingenuity in the most extravagant way.Over the last few years its popularity has sky rocketed as it continues to explore the boundaries of creativity. The extent of diversity in the events is what entices the audience on such a large scale
